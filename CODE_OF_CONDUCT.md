# Code of Conduct

This project is for learning and community knowledge sharing. Everyone participating in issues, pull requests, and discussions is expected to be respectful, constructive, and patient.

## Expected Behavior

- Be welcoming to learners at different experience levels.
- Critique ideas and explanations, not people.
- Assume good intent, but be open to correction.
- Keep technical discussions focused and useful.
- Give credit when building on someone else's work.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, threats, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language or behavior.
- Repeated off-topic comments that disrupt discussion.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Submitting copied content without proper attribution or permission.

## Enforcement

The maintainer may edit, hide, or remove comments and contributions that violate this code of conduct. Repeated or severe violations may result in being blocked from participating in the project.

If you see a problem, open an issue or contact the maintainer privately through the contact information available on the GitHub profile.
